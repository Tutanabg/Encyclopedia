ENGINEERING GEOLOGY

Engineering Geology is a branch of Geology that deals with the application of geological principles on civil engineering structures.

This field of study relay on branches of geology like petrology and Structural Geology to study the outcrops, which contains rock forming minerals, Click here to view Rock Forming Minerals where civil engineering structures like buildings, dams and roads are constructed.

It also applies engineering principles like rock and soil mechanics in addition to the application of geological principles.

Engineering Geology can be expained as one of the essential branches of geology where safety and duration of civil engineering structures relay on.
